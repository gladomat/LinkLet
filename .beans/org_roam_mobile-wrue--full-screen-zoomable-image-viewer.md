Provide a full-screen viewer for images opened from notes.

Acceptance:
- Tap inline image opens full-screen overlay.
- Pinch-to-zoom and pan (drag) supported; keep image bounded reasonably.
- Single tap toggles close (tap again closes).
- Optional: double-tap zoom preset (e.g., 1x ↔ 2x).
- Back button closes viewer.
